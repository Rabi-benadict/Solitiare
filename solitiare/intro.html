Solitaire

Objective:

The goal is to build four foundation piles, one for each suit (hearts, diamonds, clubs, and spades). Youmust build these piles from Ace to King.

Setup:

  • The game begins with the cards shuffled and dealt into seven tableau columns.
  • The first column contains one card, the second column contains two cards, and so on, until the seventh column contains seven cards.
  • The top card in each tableau column is face-up, and the remaining cards are face-down.

    • Gameplay:

  • Cards in the tableau columns can be moved onto other cards of the opposite color and one rank higher. For example, you can move a red 5 onto a black 6.
  • A sequence of cards can also be moved together as a group if they are in descending order and of the same suit. For example, you can move a red Queen onto a black King along with any other cards in descending order (e.g., black Jack, red 10).
  • Empty tableau columns can be filled with any card or group of cards.
  • Cards from the stock pile can be dealt onto the tableau columns one at a time.
  • Once all cards have been moved to the foundation piles in ascending order and sorted by suit, the game is won.

    • Controls:

  • Click and drag cards to move them.
  • Click on the deck to deal new cards onto the tableau.
  • Restart the game at any time by clicking the restart button.

    • Enjoy Your Game!

    That's all you need to know to start playing Solitaire. Have fun and good luck!
